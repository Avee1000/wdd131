Overview

Site Name: ServiceHub

I chose this site name because it represents my vision of connecting Service Providers (vendors) to customers

Domain name: shub.com

Site Purpose

Connecting, Serving and Growing: To connect users with trusted service providers in a variety of categories, and help service providers grow their businesses..

  3. Why should I use this Service Platform?
